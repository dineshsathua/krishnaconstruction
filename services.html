Water Supply &
Sanitation Infrastructure

Government-grade systems under DWSD for communities & urban networks

  • High-Capacity Water Supply Schemes
    Large-scale rural & urban drinking water projects per DWSD technical specifications. Covers intake works, treatment, storage, and distribution.
  • DI / HDPE / MS Pipeline Networks
    Ductile iron, HDPE, and MS pipeline laying for water distribution across village clusters and urban zone distribution networks.
  • Filtration & Water Treatment Plants
    Slow sand filters, sedimentation tanks, flocculation units, rapid sand filters, ESRs, GLSRs, and chlorination & disinfection systems.
  • Valve & Pump Station Installations
    Sluice valves, NRVs, air release valves, pressure gauges, butterfly valves, and complete motor pump booster station packages.
  • Underground Drainage & Sanitation
    UGD networks, individual household toilet connections, sewage treatment plants (STP), and village-level ODF sanitation infrastructure.
  • Overhead & Ground Level Storage Tanks
    RCC ESR / GLSR construction, MS overhead tanks, and balancing reservoirs sized as per demand assessment.

Building & Civil
Construction

Structural excellence for government & institutional infrastructure under BCD

  • Government Administrative Buildings
    Offices, taluk buildings, panchayat complexes, community halls per BCD technical sanction drawings and schedule of rates.
  • Structural & RCC Engineering Works
    RCC framing, retaining walls, RCC bridges, box culverts, and heavy load-bearing civil infrastructure to IS code specifications.
  • Schools, PHCs & Healthcare Facilities
    New blocks for government schools, primary health centres, district hospitals, and anganwadi centres per departmental standards.
  • Roads, Drains & Site Development
    CC/BT roads, kerb stones, storm-water drains, compound walls, open drains, site clearance, and campus development works.
  • Renovation, Repair & Retrofitting
    Structural restoration, waterproofing treatment, and maintenance of existing government-owned buildings and infrastructure assets.
  • Flooring, Finishing & MEP Works
    Internal and external plastering, tiling, painting, internal plumbing, electrical conduit, and sanitary fitting as per BCD drawings.
